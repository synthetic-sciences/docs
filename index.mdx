## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Thesis">
    Visit [thesis.syntheticsciences.ai](https://thesis.syntheticsciences.ai) and sign in. Thesis provisions your account automatically on first login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first research node">
    From the dashboard, create a root node for your research topic. Add a title, summary, and any initial content. Stage it while you work, then commit when findings are stable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your agent host">
    Run `npx --yes @synsci/thesis setup` to install the Thesis MCP server into Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, OpenCode, or any other supported host. Your agent can then read and write Thesis state through 86 structured tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The Thesis web app works without any additional configuration. The MCP/CLI integration is optional — use it to give your AI coding agents full access to your research graph.
</Note>
